# Pidgeon

Pidgeon is a secure peer-to-peer (p2p) messaging application designed for privacy and direct communication. Who you are does not matter. There are no accounts, no central servers, and no middlemen. Pidgeon connects you directly to your recipient, ensuring your messages remain private and between you and the person you are talking to.

Think of Pidgeon as your modern-day carrier pigeon.

```
        \\
         (o>
     \\_//)
      \_/_)
       _|_
```
Sending messages securely and safely, straight from you to your recipient.
